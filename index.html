Lisbon Design Hub

Located in a charming Mews, steps away from the buzzing square of the Time Out Market, this one-of-a-kind warehouse is primed to become a leading hub for all interior designers and homemakers in Lisbon. Ideally positioned among design and architectural firms and near the British School's active community, it offers a prime location with significant potential.

Our Vision

To foster a cohesive and inspiring environment, carefully curated brands under a unified direction

This approach ensures both visual harmony and conceptual synergy, allowing each brand to shine while contributing to a greater collective identity.

As part of this vision, a central shared space at the main entrance will be regularly reimagined by guest interior designers. Through rotating installations, these creatives will design immersive living environments that thoughtfully showcase and elevate the products of our participating brands.

Partners

Partners will invest by renting a space within the hub, which includes access to this central showcase area, as well as a private 10m² office or mono-brand showroom. Each brand is free to personalise and style their space to suit their identity.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide start-ups and smaller studios with an affordable yet prestigious platform—one that offers visibility, creative flexibility, and access to a high-value audience of designers, specifiers, and design-conscious clients from Lisbon and beyond.

To establish ourselves as the point of reference, the ultimate one-stop shop, and—most importantly—a leading design hub for all of Europe, we are actively seeking the very best product designers in Portugal and across the continent.

Our goal is to bring together exceptional talent under one roof to shape the future of interior design from the heart of Lisbon. To further enrich the experience, artists and local artisans will be invited to showcase their work on a rotating basis, adding depth and creativity to the space without detracting from the visibility of our core partner brands.
